Notes

1Radiative lifetimes Dagdigian, Cruse, et al., 1974 for the upper and the lower component of C 2Π(v=0) are 16.6 and 17.5 ns, respectively. Capelle, Bradford, et al., 1973 give τ = 22 ns.
